About

Hello and welcome to my site! I'm a PhD candidate and NSF Graduate Research Fellow studying detonation science for the broader purpose of enabling next-generation propulsion technology. My work pairs high-speed optical diagnostics and reduced order models to understand how detonation waves propagate, stabilize, and fail.
